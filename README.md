## 游빑 Funding Readiness Scorecard

The Funding Readiness Scorecard provides an over view for the decision:

### 游늵 Assessment Domains

| Domain | Focus Area | Readiness Indicator |
|------|-----------|--------------------|
| **Financial Readiness** | Budgeting, financial controls, projections, audit preparedness | 游댯 High / 游리 Medium / 游댮 Low |
| **Governance & Leadership** | Board structure, decision authority, policies, accountability | 游댯 High / 游리 Medium / 游댮 Low |
| **Operational Capacity** | SOPs, delivery consistency, scalability, performance tracking | 游댯 High / 游리 Medium / 游댮 Low |
| **Market Position** | Demand validation, customers, traction, competitive positioning | 游댯 High / 游리 Medium / 游댮 Low |
| **IT & Digital Enablement** | Systems usage, data integrity, cybersecurity, automation | 游댯 High / 游리 Medium / 游댮 Low |
| **Compliance & Legal** | Regulatory alignment, contracts, risk exposure | 游댯 High / 游리 Medium / 游댮 Low |

---

### 游낁 Overall Funding Readiness Classification

| Classification | Description |
|---------------|------------|
| **Funding Ready** | Institutionally sound, suitable for immediate funding consideration |
| **Conditionally Ready** | Viable with targeted remediation actions |
| **Not Funding Ready** | High institutional risk; requires material system development |

---

### 游댌 How the Scorecard Is Used

- Supports **investment committee decision-making**
- Identifies **institutional risk exposure**
- Informs **Gap Analysis and Growth Plan priorities**
- Aligns organisations with **DFI, bank, and investor expectations**

The scorecard ensures funding decisions are based on **verifiable systems, not assumptions**.
